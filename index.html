CARE Tools CQI Framework Timeline

Community Care Licensing Inspection Process Project - 2026

New CARE Tool Development & Initial Implementation

Activities for newly developed tools during rollout and first year

Timeline Activity/Task Description Responsible Party
First 3 Months Initial Feedback Collection Identify duplicative, obsolete, or conflicting regulations/statutes for possible Tool exclusion Program Policy, Project Management, QMB
First 3 Months Monthly Management Reporting Report inspection data at Division, Program, and regional office levels Quality Management Bureau
Semi-Annually (Year 1) Citation Frequency Analysis Review citation types and advisory notes frequency with monthly trends Quality Management Bureau, RADD
Semi-Annually (Year 1) Manual Citation Review Review manually cited requirements to determine Standard Tool additions Quality Management Bureau
Semi-Annually (Year 1) N/A Response Analysis Examine N/A response frequency by month to identify gateway question needs Quality Management Bureau
Semi-Annually (Year 1) PCA Confirmatory Analysis Conduct Principal Components Analysis to validate domain trigger rules RADD, Quality Management Bureau
Semi-Annually (Year 1) Citation Consistency Review Examine citation and advisory note consistency across inspections Quality Management Bureau
After First Year New Requirements Review (1-Year) Review citation frequency for newly added requirements; determine Standard Tool, Domain Focused Tool, or removal based on frequency and SME criticality ratings Quality Management Bureau, SMEs
After First Year Gateway Question Development Determine gateway question additions/modifications based on N/A trends to improve inspection efficiency Quality Management Bureau, SMEs
After First Year Tool Content Validation Validate tool requirements using citation frequency data and SME criticality ratings Quality Management Bureau, SMEs
After First Year Initial LPA & Licensee Surveys Conduct post-inspection surveys to assess initial CARE Tool user experience Quality Management Bureau

Ongoing CARE Tool Maintenance & CQI

Continuous quality improvement activities for existing CARE Tools

Frequency Activity/Task Description Responsible Party
Quarterly Program Coordination Meeting Review data analysis, identify barriers, evaluate legislation, update work plans Program Coordination Workgroup
Annual Standard Tool Updates Update tools for new requirements and emerging health/safety concerns Program Coordination Workgroup
Annual Comprehensive Data Analysis Review citations, advisory notes, manual citations, domain analysis, interview responses, deficiency distribution, and survey data Quality Management Bureau, RADD
Annual Violation Consistency Analysis Identify citation and advisory note inconsistencies across inspections; determine if due to case differences or inconsistent practices Quality Management Bureau
Annual LPA & Licensee Surveys Conduct post-inspection surveys on CARE Tools efficiency and user experience Quality Management Bureau
Triennial Comprehensive CQI Review Re-evaluate triggers, correlations, Tool content, and training based on data analysis Quality Management Bureau, RADD, SMEs
Triennial New Requirements Review (3-Year) Review citation trends for all requirements added in prior three years; determine Standard versus Domain Focused Tool placement Quality Management Bureau, SMEs
Triennial Inter-Rater Reliability Studies Identify domains and requirements with low inter-rater agreement using concurrent shadow inspections Quality Management Bureau, Field Staff
Triennial Trend Analysis Examine citation/advisory trends, domain triggers, N/A usage, and interview responses Quality Management Bureau, RADD
Triennial SME Workgroup Evaluations Evaluate tool organization and content; conduct efficiency assessment; recommend improvements Subject Matter Experts, Program Staff
Note: New CARE Tools follow the implementation timeline before transitioning to ongoing maintenance. The 1-Year Review evaluates newly added requirements individually after first year inclusion, while the 3-Year Review examines all requirements added during the prior three years as part of comprehensive triennial CQI.
